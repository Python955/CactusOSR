# **CactusOSR**
## CactusOSR is currently in open-beta. Dual-boot support is not yet implemented. For now, use a USB drive to run the operating system.
# How to install CactusOSR:
## Linux:
### 1. Download latest release. (NOT RELEASED YET)
### 2. Open your gparted on linux. (Windows partitons next paragraph)
### 3. Open your usb on gparted.
### 4. Resize your partition (or remove at least 10mb out of your usb)
### 5. Make a new partition on that unlocated space
### 6. Press the green check on the top of gparted
### 7. Change the new partitions flags to: Boot, ESP
### 8. Open the .ISO file and move insides to USB. 6 GB is reccomended
### 9. Boot the usb
## 100 MB is needed but 6gb is recoomended.
# Windows directions:
### 1. Open disk management
### 2. Right-click on your usb drive and resize at least 10mb from it
### 3. Add a new partition on the unlocated space
### 4. Extract the .ISO contents onto the USB drive.
## 100 MB is needed but 6gb is recoomended.

# For booting:
### It depends on your motherboard. Check online or a youtube video on how to boot a usb drive
